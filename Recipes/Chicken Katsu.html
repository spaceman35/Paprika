Chicken Katsu

Japanese

Ingredients

Chicken:

4 chicken breasts or thighs

2 cups (290g) All purpose flour

3 Eggs

2tbsp (30ml) water

2 cups (210g) Panko

2 cups oil

salt or miso to taste (miso is very salty)


Cabbage Slaw:

1/4 head cabbage thinly sliced

1/2 bunch green onion thinly sliced

salt to taste

lemon juice to taste

spicy chili garlic topping to taste

Directions

Season chicken with salt overnight (or miso!)
Dip in flour, then egg, then panko
Fry in veg oil till crispy golden brown
Put on wire racks to cool

Slaw:
Just mix up the cabbage slaw and really squeeze it to break it down a bit
Serve with bulldog sauce on bread with crusts cut off!
