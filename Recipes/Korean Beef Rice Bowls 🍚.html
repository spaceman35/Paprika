Korean Beef Rice Bowls 🍚

Korean, Lunch

Servings: 4

Ingredients

- 4 Tbsps Soy Sauce

- 1/2 Tbsp Rice Vinegar

- 1/2 Tbsp Sesame Oil

- 1 Tbsp Minced Garlic

- 1 Tbsp Gochujang Paste

- 2 Tbsps Brown Sugar

- 24oz Lean Ground Beef (96/4)

- 1 Cup White Rice (Dry)

- 1 1/2 Cups Chicken Bone Broth

- Fresh Green Onions (Optional)

- Sesame Seeds (Optional)


Sriracha Mayo Ingredients:

- 4 Tbsps Light Mayo

- 2 Tbsps Sriracha

- 2 Tbsps Lemon Juice

- Garlic Salt

Directions

combine sauce ingredients

brown beef, add sauce when nearly done cooking

cook rice with bone broth for extra protein

top rice with the marinated beef, chopped green onions, and sriracha mayo (squirt using a squeeze bottle)

Nutrition

450 Calories
45g Protein
48g Carbs
10g Fat
