Chicken Fried Rice

Dinner

Prep Time: 5 mins Cook Time: 10 mins Servings: 4 Source: allrecipes.com

Ingredients

1 large egg

1 tablespoon water

1 tablespoon butter

1 tablespoon vegetable oil

1 onion, chopped

2 cups cooked white rice, cold

1 cup cooked, chopped chicken meat

2 tablespoons soy sauce

1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions

Gather all ingredients.

Beat egg and water together in small bowl.
Melt butter in a large skillet or wok over medium-low heat
Add egg and cook without stirring, 1 to 2 minutes.
Remove egg from skillet and cut into shreds; set aside.

Heat oil in the same skillet.
Add onion; cook and stir until soft, about 3 minutes.
Stir in rice, chicken, soy sauce, and pepper.
Cook and stir for about 5 minutes; stir in shredded egg.
Serve hot.
