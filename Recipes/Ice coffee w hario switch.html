Ice coffee w/ hario switch

Coffee

Ingredients

28g coffee, fine-medium to medium grind

150 grams of ice

Melitta #4 coffee filter

Clever dripper

Gooseneck pouring kettle

Filtered water at optimal brewing temp of 195 - 205° F

Decanter large enough to brew into

Scale

Timer

Directions

Weigh out 28 grams of coffee

16 ounces (or around 300 mL) of filtered water, heated to the optimal brewing temperature of 195 - 205° F

add coffee, tare scale

50 g of water bloom

@ 45 seconds, pour water until 300g total
~2 min pour

@ 2:30, pour over 150g ice
fill with additional ice as needed
