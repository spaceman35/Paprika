쑥 쉬폰 케이크 (mugwort cake)

Deserts

Source: Youtube.com

Ingredients

Egg Yolks 80g

White Sugar 25g

Canola Oil 50g

Water 70g

Full Cream Milk 20g

Plain Flour 70g

Baking Powder 2g

Mugwort Powder 15g

Egg Whites 200g

White Sugar 60g


Roasted soy bean cream:

30g whipping cream

10g roasted soy bean powder

200g whipping cream

20g sugar

Directions

soy bean cream:
mix 30g cream w/ 10g (sifted) soy bean powder
add remaining cream + sugar and mix until stiff peaks
