Neapolitan Pizza with King Arthur Flour

Pizza

Source: Youtube.com

Ingredients

Day 1 - Poolish:

300g water

2g yeast

300g flour


Day 2 -

350g room temp water

poolish

700g 00 pizza flower

20g salt

Directions

Day 1 - Mix poolish ingredients, rest covered 24hr @ room temperature

Day 2 - in stand mixer
mix ingredients, mix 10 mins on low speed
cover in mixer bowl for 1 hr at room temp
portion it up into tight balls, refrigerate for 24 hrs

Day 3 - get to room temp, stretch out, then make pizza!
